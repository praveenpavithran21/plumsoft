PlumCore ERP Overview

PlumCore ERP is an intelligent, all-in-one solution designed to automate and optimize your enterprise operations. Whether you're managing sales pipelines or tracking production on the floor, PlumCore adapts to your workflows, not the other way around.

Key Modules

  • Finance & Accounting: GST, TDS, bank reconciliation, fund flow
  • CRM & Sales: Lead-to-order management, dashboards
  • Purchase & Inventory: Vendor quotes, POs, GRNs, stock tracking
  • Production Planning: MRP, BOM, job cards, WIP
  • Fixed Assets: Depreciation, asset tracking
  • Quality & Maintenance: Preventive & corrective actions
  • HR & Payroll (Add-on): Employee lifecycle, payroll, compliance

AI-Enhanced Features

  • WhatsApp-based approvals & commands
  • Resume & RFP parsing using NLP
  • Dynamic dashboards with drill-down
  • Offline field operations with JSON sync
